How to Play

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Move in a direction
  • Bomb: Space (if you have bombs)
  • Heal: H (if you have heals)
  • Pause: P
  • Fullscreen: Click fullscreen button (top right)
  • Pickups: Walk over items to collect
  • Character: Select to have different stats
Defeat all enemies to open doors. Beat the boss to advance!
